## Eranorms: Historical Weather & Climate

Explore weather history, spot climate trends, and get context for your local weather

Is today actually hotter than usual? Do we really get more rain on weekends? Eranorms answers these questions by putting daily weather into deep historical context. Visualize climate trends with beautiful, data-rich charts and compare the current year against decades of history.

### Context is Everything
• **Daily Distribution**: See how today's temperature ranks against every year since 1950.
• **Cumulative Data**: Track rainfall and snow accumulation vs. normal.
• **Myth Busters**: Test local weather tropes against hard data.
• **Lifestyle Metrics**: "Shorts Days", "Coats Days", and "Perfect Days" counts.

### Visualize the Climate
• **Climate Stripes**: See warming/cooling trends at a glance.
• **Heatmaps**: Spot anomalies across months and years.
• **Box Plots**: Understand the spread of "normal" for any time of year.
• **ENSO Analysis**: See how El Niño and La Niña affect your local weather.

### Deep Dive
• **Station Data**: Access high-quality data from ASOS stations (US/Canada/Intl).
• **Custom Baselines**: Compare against the 1991-2020 normal or set your own.
• **Export & Share**: Share beautiful charts and detailed stats.
